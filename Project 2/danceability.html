Danceability Analysis






  The data shows that, while there is not a definite relationship between danceability and popularity, more danceable tracks tend to have a higher popularity score. The majority of the tracks that were analyzed have danceability ratings above 0.6, and most of those are in the upper range of popularity. However, there is a non-negligible amount of songs that have danceability scores below 0.5 but are still popular. There are also a few songs that are very danceable, but are not popular.
