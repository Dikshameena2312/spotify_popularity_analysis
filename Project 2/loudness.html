Loudness Analysis






There appears to be a relationship between loudness (measured in decibels) and popularity. Most of the plot points are in the upper range or loudness with popularity scores above 60. However, this may be due to recording techniques that increase the decibel level of a track, rather than an attribute inherent to the song itself, as this has been a trend in the music industry since the 1990's.
